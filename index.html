A Thoughtful Destination

Lounge Here offers a unique, thoughtful destination away from the ordinary. Owned by Julie Doyle, co-founder of musical groups Tripping Daisy and The Polyphonic Spree, the lounge provides an inviting and chic atmosphere. The decor features a large photo mural by Rainer Judd, adding a touch of Marfa to the space. Our Southern-inspired food menu and seasonal craft cocktails aim to provide an adventurous and special experience.
