Lounge Here offers a unique, thoughtful destination away from the ordinary. Owned by Julie Doyle, co-founder of musical groups Tripping Daisy and The Polyphonic Spree, and Good Records, the lounge provides an inviting and chic atmosphere. The decor features a large photo mural by Rainer Judd, adding a touch of Marfa to the space. The Southern-inspired food menu includes sharable small plates like Deviled Eggs and Pimento Cheese, and entrees such as Shrimp and Grits and Chicken Fried Akaushi Ribeye. The cocktail menu is a collaboration of favorite lounge bartenders, changing with the seasons to keep things fresh.
